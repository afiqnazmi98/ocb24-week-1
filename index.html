To My Beloved Tummy


Oh, dearest tummy, soft and round and true,
In your embrace, I find my joy anew.
Your gentle curve, your tender, warm embrace,
A comforting, beloved, cherished space.

From dawn till dusk, you hold me close and tight,
A faithful friend, a beacon in the night.
In times of hunger, you are ever kind,
A source of solace, peace, and love entwined.

When life is busy and the days are long,
You are the melody in my life's song.
Each rumble speaks of secrets only we know,
A tender whisper in the ebb and flow.

In moments sweet and simple, you delight,
With every bite, you make my heart take flight.
Oh, how you dance with every tasty treat,
A celebration in each bite we meet.

When worries come and shadows start to creep,
You cradle me in solace, help me sleep.
Through every up and down, you're always there,
A steadfast presence, filled with love and care.

So here's my ode, my heartfelt love for you,
My cherished tummy, steadfast and true.
For in your warmth and softness, I confide,
My faithful friend, my comfort and my guide.

With every meal and every tender touch,
I vow to cherish you, to love so much.
